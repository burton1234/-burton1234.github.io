Site Purpose

This site is for the information of ANY Town USA. Information will be provided by photo and displays with description and explanation of the photos and displays. It will have maps of the town. This site will provide information about the town and local events taking place.

Target audience will be primarily female/male age 16-65, but we will appeal to families as will.
